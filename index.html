About Us


The Strand is an organization made up of people united in creating Custom Commands and Modules. Founded 07/14/2018, our team includes people from France, America, Singapore, and all over the world; all connected by our love for innovation and our passion for creativity. We're passionate about pushing the limits of what can be achieved with Custom Commands in Dynobot and invite you to join us, whether it be by trying out our modules or submitting your own creations.

You can find more information about each of our Staff and their interests on the Staff Page below.

Disclaimer: While Strand provides Custom Commands and Modules for free use, we are not connected to Dynobot as an official subdivision or sponsored by them.
