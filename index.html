About Us


The Strand is an organization made up of people united in creating Custom Commands and Modules for the Discord Bot Dyno. Founded in July 2018, our team includes people from America, Singapore, and all over the world, all connected by our love for innovation and our passion for creativity. We're innovative with our techniques, using them to create new and cutting edge commands. We're passionate about pushing the limits of what can be achieved with Custom Commands in Dyno and invite you to join us, whether it be by trying out our modules and commands, or by submitting your own commands and creations.

You can find more information about each of our team members and their interests on the team member page below.

Disclaimer: While the Strand team provides Custom Commands and Modules for free use, we are not connected to the Dyno team as an official subdivision nor sponsored by them in any way, shape, or form.
